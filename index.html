BO4 or formally know as Call of Duty Black Ops 4, it is part of the gaming line of all Call of Duty's made by Activision. They have all had a common genre of shooter. The games modes span from a survival mode called zombies were you try and fight off zombies as long as you can, to a new game mode called blackout where players will have to scavenge, strategise, compete and survive to win. It features the largest map that's ever been built in Call of Duty.

Fortnite battle royal on the other hand is a fairly new game coming out only last fall as a complete game mode. It has almost the same format as Blackout except for one thing, the animated graphics.
